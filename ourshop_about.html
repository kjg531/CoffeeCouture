About us

We proudly brew Coffee, which is organic and locally roasted. The beans come from 4 different continents to create just the right blend of flavor to help you start your morning!

We bake all of our pastries in house fresh and ready every morning. Try one of our homemade muffins, croissants, or scones and we are sure you will be back!

Serve Deli Meats & Cheeses in all our sandwiches & salads. Boars Head meats have no fillers or added preservatives- just clean meat! They are a MUCH healthier alternative to processed deli meats.

At our shop we provide free Wi-Fi and beautiful outdoor seating.We also specialize in Espresso Bar Catering Service and BoarsHead Deli Catering Service!
